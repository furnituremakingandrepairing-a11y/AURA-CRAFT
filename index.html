Who we are

One workshop. Two product lines. Zero compromise.

Aura Craft International was founded by Akhand Yadav with a single belief: that good furniture shouldn't require choosing between quality and cost. From our factory in Ja Mata Di Compound, Kalher, Thane, we manufacture every piece ourselves — no outsourcing, no shortcuts.

We offer two routes to a furnished home. Our ready-made collection is photographed, priced, and ready to ship. Our custom division accepts commissions for pieces built to your exact dimensions, material choices, and finish preferences — from a single bedside table to a full interior fitout.

What we stand for

The principles behind every joint

Factory-direct quality

We manufacture what we sell. No middlemen, no margins layered on top — which means you get better wood for the same rupees.

Handcrafted joinery

Our craftsmen use traditional joinery techniques — mortise and tenon, dovetail joints — alongside modern finishing, so your furniture doesn't wobble in five years.

Pan-India delivery

We've shipped to 28+ states. Every piece is packed with professional-grade crating and delivered to your door with assembly assistance available.
